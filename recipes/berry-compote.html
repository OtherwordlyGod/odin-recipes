Berry Compote

Description

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker
  3. Cover and cook on High until bubbling, about 1 1/2 hours
  4. Stif cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Stir into berry mixture. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5 to 10 minuets.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature
  6. Enjoy!
