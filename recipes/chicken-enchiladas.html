Slow Cooked Chicken Enchilada Soup

Description

Chicken enchilada soup is a tasty and hot meal. Topped with crushed corn chips, shredded cheese, and chopped green onions. Perfect for cold days with just the right amount of tang.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 15.25 ounce can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 14.5 ounce can enchilada soup
  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 4 ounce can diced gree chiles
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Rinse chicken breasts and pat dry
  3. Place chicken into bottom of a slow cooker
  4. Add corn, tomatoes, chicken broth, enchilada soup, onion, green chiles, cilantro, garlic, bay leaves, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper
  5. Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours
  6. Transfer chicken to a large plate, then shred meat with two forks.
  7. Return shredded chicken to the slow cooker,stir to combine, and contiue cooking for 30-60 minuets.
  8. Discard bay leaves before serving
  9. Enjoy!
