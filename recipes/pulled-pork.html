Slow Cooked Pulled-Pork Buns

Description

pulled pork that is cooked with BBQ sauce and root beer then sandwiched between two toasted buns. Simple but delicious and melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of pork tenderloin
  • 12 fluid ounce can or bottle of root beer
  • 18 fluid ounce bottle of barbeque sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns, split and lightely toasted

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Place the pork tenderloin in a slow cooker: pour root beer over the top
  3. Cover and cook on Low until pork shreds easily, 6-7 hours
  4. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce
  5. Serve on hamburger buns
  6. Enjoy!
